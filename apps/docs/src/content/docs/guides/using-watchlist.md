---
title: Using the Watchlist
description: How to save and track accounts and assets.
---

The Watchlist lets you save accounts and assets you want to monitor. Data is stored locally in your browser.

## Adding to the Watchlist

On any account or asset page, click the bookmark icon to add it to your watchlist.

## Viewing Your Watchlist

Navigate to the Watchlist page from the sidebar. Your saved items are organized by type:

- **Accounts** — Saved public keys with their current XLM balance
- **Assets** — Saved assets with their code and issuer

## Managing Items

Click the bookmark icon again to remove an item. Your watchlist is stored in your browser's local storage and persists across sessions.

:::tip
The watchlist is per-browser. Switching browsers or clearing browser data will reset it.
:::
