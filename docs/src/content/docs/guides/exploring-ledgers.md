---
title: Exploring Ledgers
description: How to browse ledgers on the Stellar network.
---

A ledger is a snapshot of the Stellar network state at a point in time. New ledgers close approximately every 5 seconds.

## Browsing Ledgers

The Ledgers page shows recent ledgers in reverse chronological order. Each ledger card displays:

- **Sequence number** — The ledger's position in the chain
- **Closed at** — When the ledger was finalized
- **Transaction count** — How many transactions were included
- **Operation count** — Total operations across all transactions

## Ledger Details

Click a ledger to see its full details and the list of transactions it contains.

## Live Updates

The explorer streams new ledgers in real-time. The latest ledger appears automatically at the top of the list without refreshing the page.
